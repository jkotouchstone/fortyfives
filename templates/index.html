How to Play 45's

45's is a trick-taking card game with unique trump and off‑suit rankings.

Bidding: Your bid is based on “top cards” in your hand: a lone 5 forces a bid of 20, and having at least two key cards (Jack, Ace of Hearts, non‑Hearts Ace, or King) also results in a bid of 20.

Trump: The trump suit is determined by the winning bidder. Remember that the Ace of Hearts is always trump.

Trick Play: If any trump card is played, the highest trump wins the trick; otherwise, the highest card of the lead suit wins.

Instruction Mode: When enabled, you'll receive extra feedback after your moves comparing your decision to an optimal strategy.

Enjoy learning and playing 45's!
