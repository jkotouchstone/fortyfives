How to Play 45's

45's is a trick-taking card game with unique trump and off‑suit rankings.

Bidding: Your bid is based on “top cards” in your hand.

Trump: The trump suit is determined by the winning bidder.

Trick Play: Each trick every player plays one card.

Dealer: The dealer rotates among players.

Instruction Mode: When enabled, you’ll receive extra feedback comparing your move to an optimal strategy.

Enjoy learning and playing 45's!
