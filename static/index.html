Detailed Rules & Strategies

Overview:
This is a trick-taking game where you bid and play cards to win tricks. Follow suit if possible; otherwise, play a trump.

Bidding:
Place your bid (15, 20, 25, or 30). The computer bids based on hand strength.

Trick Play:
The winner of each trick leads the next round. Review the game log to see all actions.
