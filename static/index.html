Welcome to 45's Game Tutorial

45's is a trick-taking game with unique trump and off‑suit rankings.

  1. Bidding: Based on the top cards in your hand, you bid a number of points. A lone "5" or multiple key cards may force higher bids.
  2. Trump Selection: The winner of the bid chooses the trump suit.
  3. Kitty Phase: After bidding, you combine your hand with three extra cards (the kitty) and select which cards to keep.
  4. Draw Phase: You then draw cards to complete your hand.
  5. Trick Play: Each trick, both you and the computer play a card. The highest card (based on trump and suit) wins the trick.
